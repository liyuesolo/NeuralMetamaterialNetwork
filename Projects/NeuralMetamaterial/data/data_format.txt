first : #1 to #of tiling parameters
then : Green_strain(0, 0) Green_strain(1, 1) Green_strain(0, 1)
then : secondPK_stress(0, 0) secondPK_stress(1, 1) secondPK_stress(0, 0)

then : direction in theta, strain, strain in the orthogonal direction
then : residual norm in simulation
